about me

Hello, I’m Jaswant, a passionate visual storyteller specializing in cinematography, video editing, and VFX. My journey began in the 6th grade, sparking a lifelong passion for capturing the world through my lens. With over 6 years of professional experience and a degree in VFX and Animation from Seamedu Institute, Pune, my work has been honored with multiple awards, including contributions to award-winning short films.

15+ YEARS OF EXPERIENCE
200+ REPEATED CLIENTS
478 COMPLETED PROJECTS
350+ HAPPY CLIENTS

I love to travel and have done a variety of work globally trying to showcase the hidden gems through my Lens, which continually fuels my creativity and provides fresh perspectives for my work. I am proficient in Premiere, After Effects, and Photoshop, and have a broad knowledge of other VFX-related software.

Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to work across a variety of industries, including fashion, industrial, corporate, influencer marketing, product photography, interior design, advertisements, events, and government projects. Notably, I have been involved in video editing for the Indian Army for the past 2-3 years and conducted editing workshops at T-Series.

Whether it’s creating content for brands or working on high-profile projects, my goal is to bring a unique and compelling vision to every project I undertake.
